Canadian Youth Climate Action Award

Pivot Green, Canada

Amount

$500 - $5000

Deadline

October 1

One of our primary goals is to support young Canadians working together with other generations on positive, impactful ideas and actions that help address climate anxiety and the climate change challenges of our times. To support this objective, Pivot Green, in partnership with the Small Change Fund, has created the Canadian Youth Climate Action Award.

Learn more about this grant

Amount

$500 - $5000

Grant Requirements

Below are the requirements that must be met for this grant.

Is primarily a youth-led, local, and community-based initiative. Involves a range of ages in its outreach and activities. Has positive environmental and social impacts. Leads to concrete, climate-friendly changes. Has the potential to be replicated in other Canadian communities.
